Apathy is Boring (AisB) is a non-partisan organization that educates and encourages youth to become active citizens in Canada's democracy. Through programs like RISE, BUILD, and Table Topics, AisB helps young people develop the skills, confidence, and connections needed to lead community projects, engage with local issues, and shape the future of their cities.
