For Our Kids Alberta is a  grassroots-style organization founded by parents. They are currently advocating for climate education and electric school buses. They meet virtually on the last Thursday of each month at 7:30 MT.

They are open to having anyone who is passionate about kids and climate change.

They also have local groups. The Calgary group is open to  grandparents/great aunts/uncles. Meeting information is unknown.
