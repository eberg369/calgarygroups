We meet in-person in Hillhurst. Black identified folks, please reach out to tyson.bankert@gmail.com to express interest. Honour our Ancestor each month. Come with a piece of their work to share. Praise. Critique. Learn. Discuss. Each month the group focuses on one person/moment or dynamic that is related to the Black experience. How we focus is by bringing material, media, whether it's poetry, video clips, art, that expresses our thoughts, ideas and on the subject/person. We then share and discuss by the end of our time together what we have learned and  have a stronger sense for our Ancestors.
